### Standard Form  
$$  
M(x,y)\,dx + N(x,y)\,dy = 0  
$$  
  
---  
  
 **Step 1 — Check Exactness**  
Condition:  
$$  
\frac{\partial M}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial N}{\partial x}  
$$  
  
- If TRUE → Exact  
- If FALSE → Not exact (need IF)  
  If not exact follow [[NonExactDE]]
---  
  
**Step 2 — General soultion \(y\)**  
$$  
y=\int M dx + \int (\text{non-x in N})\,dy+C
$$
