- Write its limitations

- Solve problems on:
  - Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL)
  - Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law (KVL)

  - Resistance (R)

- Amplitude  
- Time period  
- Frequency  
- Instantaneous value  
- Peak-to-peak value

- Explain the power triangle  
- Importance of power factor

### 8. Impedance
